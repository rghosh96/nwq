let's talk ab duty

[ there's a lot that goes on w being on duty, so make sure u kno what ur doing!!! ]


[i] *normal* duty day


duty procedure:

☑ Pick up phone at 4PM from desk on THAT day

☑ Leave phone behind desk at 8AM on the NEXT day

❗ EX: Ellie's on duty Tuesday. That means she picks the phone up from desk at 4PM on TUESDAY, and leaves it behind desk at 8AM on WEDNESDAY.


> first round | between 4-6PM

> second round | between 8-10PM

> third round | after 12AM

important duty rules:

☑ You MUST be in the dorm 8PM - 8AM on your duty day!

☑ You MUST respond to a call within 10 minutes!

☑ You CANNOT work a desk shift if u are on duty! If u have a desk shift, you MUST switch w someone!

duty reminders:

☑ You are on normal duty once a month in NWQ.

☑ Keep the phone charged!



[ii] weekend duty


duty reminders:

☑ You will have one duty weekend a semester in NWQ.

☑ There are secondaries on weekends!! so u will always hve a buddy on ur duty weekend :) <3



[iii] break duty


duty reminders:

☑ NWQ is open during both winter AND spring break

❗ Hence, there are TWO break duties

☑ We team up with Hotz & Holcombe/Futrall in order to cover these duty days.

❗ EVERYONE is required to go to the break duty meeting!!! Even if you do not plan on working during break!!!

☑ You get a $25/day stipend for each day u are on break duty.

☑ BREAK DUTY IS 24 HOURS. You PHYSICALLY pass the phone off to the next person on duty!
